Sign up for Wine night!

Experience the flavors of Taniti with an unforgettable wine tasting night on the island. Surrounded by swaying palms and ocean breezes, guests can sample a curated selection of local and international wines, each paired with artisan cheeses and tropical fruits. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or a casual sipper, the relaxed island atmosphere, stunning sunset views, and warm hospitality make it the perfect setting to swirl, sip, and savor. Cheers to a night of elegance and island charm!
